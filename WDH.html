This campaign is set in the city of waterdeep home of gangs and adventurers, aswell as our group of heroes who are travelling the city taking out the local gang one person at the time. This adventuring troop consists of a Jedi, a druid, a wizard and something else. with only one experienced player they travel through the city attempting to use what little wit they have. On their way they have encountered many intresting people and are set to meet many more.
